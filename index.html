About Bora Aymete

I'm a budding music producer and composer who is a white collar IT worker by day and a composer at night, I'm just starting my journey but with a strong passion for creating. My ears are always open for new sounds, and I'm dedicated to learning the craft of production and composition. I'm actively building my skills in digital audio workstations (DAWs) and music theory, eager to turn my ideas into polished tracks. While I'm still developing my portfolio, I'm committed to putting in the work to create compelling and unique music. I'm excited about the possibilities and ready to collaborate with fellow artists to bring fresh sounds to life.
